About Me

I graduated from School of Artificial IntelligenceofBeijing Normal University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Technology in July 2022.

My research interests mainly include the application and improvement of natural language processing methods, mainly including document-level information extraction and mining:
1) Applying good models to task processes, such as combining existing NER methods with Regex to extract textual information more efficiently and comprehensively;
2) Evaluate and improve existing models to make the whole task process perform better, such as modifying the RE model to make it more efficient and accurate;
3) Propose new task processes with more generalization capability.
